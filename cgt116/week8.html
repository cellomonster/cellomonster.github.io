CGT 116 - Week 8 - Advanced unit blocks

Advanced unit blocks with baked lighting experiment. Block meshes are combined and materials & lighting are baked to a texture in a separate UV space. This technique is only valid for small scenes like this. Some small artifacts exist in the bake. These are likely due to using a quick auto-unwrapper for the baking UVs and using a generic denoising tool on the bake.

Sculptures by David Umemoto inspired the interlocking arches

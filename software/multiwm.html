iOS Multi-window-manager

A jailbreak tweak for iPads enabling desktop-like multitasking. Unlike other window-manager tweaks for iPad, this tweak didn't just shrink the application in a window, but instead dynamically changed its layout to fit in a window. I ultimately abandoned the tweak due to Apple adding more advanced multitasking options to iPads, making the tweak redundant.

Website info

This site was written from scratch, half out of being too prideful to use a template, and half out of wanting to learn to build sites. It likely breaks many conventions and best practices in web design, but its functional.

I have a bone to pick with lots of modern websites. They try to act like apps or fancy slideshows, sacrificing ease of navigation for aesthetics and leading to the power-hungry behemoths that are modern web browsers. We've even gotten to the point where webpages and apps are the same thing! Webpages are supposed to be like documents; mostly static with a focus on content. I built this site to reflect those ideals.

I use exljbris Font Foundry's Museo Slab for heading text and Sebastian Kosch's Crimson Text for everything else. I don't know if these font choices obey whatever typography fundamentals in web design there are. I just eyeballed it, thinking they look nice :)
